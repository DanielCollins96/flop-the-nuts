Texas Hold'em Flop Trainer

Flop the Nuts is an interactive poker hand calculator for studying Texas Hold'em boards. Generate a random flop or build a custom board to see the current nuts, the strongest two-card hand tiers, combo counts, board texture, flush draws, straight draws, and estimated showdown equity.

Use it to practice flop strategy, compare made hands with redraws, and test how your hole cards perform against one to eight random opponents on the flop, turn, or river.

What does Flop the Nuts calculate?

Flop the Nuts ranks every legal two-card Texas Hold'em hand for the current board, identifies the nut hand, groups matching hand tiers, and estimates showdown equity.

Can I choose a custom poker flop?

Yes. You can click board cards to replace them, add turn and river cards, and choose your own two-card hand for equity estimates.
